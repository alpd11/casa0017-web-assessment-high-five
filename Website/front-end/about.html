One Biker -- A website gives you
clear information about cycling in London

Our project aims to analyze the impact of weather conditions on bicycle traffic trends across various UK regions from 2010 to 2020. Our group will combine the UK Met Office Weather Data API with the UK Department for Transport Bicycle Traffic Data to analyze this issue. This website will present how weather factors, such as temperature, precipitation, and wind speed, influence cycling behavior, therefore providing valuable information for urban planning, public health, and sustainability policy initiatives.
